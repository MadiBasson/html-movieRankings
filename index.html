The Best Movies According to Madi

My top 3 movies of all time.

Hereditary

I am a big horror fan and this one has always stuck with me. It is disturbing and dark and always comes to mind when I need to recommend scary movies to friends.

The Upside

I loved this movie. Kevin Heart acts in it but it is just a funny feel good movie. It makes you realise everyone is constantly working through something and that there is still always light at the end of the tunnel.

Brightburn

Horror and soppy movies to a good super hero gone rogue movie. It's all me. This movie is so good, because I personally think it portrais what actually will happen should someone end up having superpowers. Leaving a scorched earth for us mortals.
